About me

I'm a Software Developer and Creative Technologist based in Pretoria, Gauteng, South Africa. My work lives at the intersection of functional software engineering and digital creative design — I build applications that are not only technically solid but visually compelling.

With a strong foundation in Android development (Kotlin & Java), web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), database management (SQL), and game development (Unity & C#), I bring a rare combination of coding proficiency and creative vision to every project.
